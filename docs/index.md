# Introduction

Thanks for visiting this site. We are currently focused on building guides and publishing release notes. More content is on the way, please check back soon.

Please visit the [Common hosted Single Sign on
(CSS) App](https://sso-requests.apps.gold.devops.gov.bc.ca/) if you are looking for integration with a login component. The process is completely self-serve and takes only minutes. You’ll need an IDIR account with MFA enabled, and you _should_ be a product owner, product admin, or team lead for the project. Once automated provisioning is complete, your client details will be securely available through the web app. 

**(\*) Note for BCeID and BCSC Requests**

The Pathfinder SSO team will provision your DEV and TEST clients immediately. Client names and secrets for these environments will be shared through a secure channel.
Your PROD client will be set up after approval is received from the IDIM team.
