### Accessing the Common Hosted Single Sign-On (CSS) App

Working with your product owner or technical contact, you can get started by submitting a request through the CSS App.

Use the [Common Hosted Single Sign on (CSS) App](https://sso-requests.apps.gold.devops.gov.bc.ca/) to request integration. For IDIR with MFA integrations, the process is fully self‑serve and typically takes only a few minutes. 

To access the app, you’ll need:
- An IDIR account with MFA enabled  
- A role as a product owner, product admin, or team lead for the project

Once automated provisioning is complete, your client details will be securely available through the web application.
